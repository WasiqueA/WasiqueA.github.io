Internet users data

Internet users are individuals who have used the Internet (from any location) in the last 3 months. The Internet can be used via a computer, mobile phone, personal digital assistant, games machine, digital TV etc.

This charts shows internet users for USA, Canada & United Kingdom for Year 2013 to 2016. First chart shows total internet users for USA, Canada & United Kingdom for Year 2013 to 2016.

Second chart shows internet users per 100 users for each country (USA, Canada & United Kingdom) for Year 2013 to 2016
